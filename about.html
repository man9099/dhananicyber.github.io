About Dhanani Cyber

Dhanani Cyber is a cybersecurity consulting firm serving small and mid-sized businesses across the United States.

We help organizations understand their cyber risk, make informed decisions, and strengthen their security posture in a practical and sustainable way.

Our Philosophy

Effective security should be understandable, prioritized, and aligned with how a business operates.

We focus on clarity, transparency, and actionable guidance rather than fear-driven messaging or unnecessary complexity.

What Clients Can Expect

Professional assessments, objective recommendations, and clear communication throughout the engagement.

Advisory services only. No legal advice or guarantees of security outcomes.
